About Alan

Built from the Inside Out

Alan Goodman is a Milwaukee-based Fractional COO, operational excellence strategist, and national keynote speaker whose career spans Fortune 500 boardrooms, entrepreneurial ventures, and transformational consulting engagements serving organizations across manufacturing, education, professional services, and beyond.


As a GE-certified Lean Six Sigma Black Belt and graduate of the Jack Welch Leadership Institute at Crotonville, Alan brings an elite operational framework to every engagement. He is a national keynote speaker, AARP Make Your Move grant winner, and White House Business Briefing invitee whose story has been featured in BizTimes Milwaukee, the Milwaukee Journal Sentinel, and on WISN-TV ABC.


As founder and principal of A Goodman's Consulting LLC, Alan serves as a Fractional COO for growing businesses — embedding with leadership teams to build the systems, culture, and operational capacity needed to scale with purpose.


Alan is also an active civic leader — serving as an elected board member of Crime Stoppers of Milwaukee and Commissioner on the Milwaukee County Commission on Aging, appointed positions he holds as an extension of his commitment to community impact.
